Types of Coffee

There are an incredible number of coffee varieties in the world. They differ in composition, taste, aroma. These qualities primarily depend on the type of coffee trees and where they grow. There are more than 90 species of coffee trees that provide seeds for the drink beloved by many. But there are only 3 main ones: Arabica (Coffea Arabica); Robusta (Coffea Canephora); other varieties.
